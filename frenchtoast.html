Masala Dosa

Breakfast Dish

Total Calories: 149 calories
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup dosa batter
    		•
  • 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
    		•
  • ½ tsp turmeric powde
    		•
  • ½ tsp chana dal, 1 tbsp urad dal
  • 1 tbsp oil,salt to taste
    		•
Method:
Procedure
Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, curry leaves, and green chili. Add onions, turmeric powder, and salt. Sauté till onions are soft. Mix in mashed potatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes. Heat a dosa pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread thinly. Place a portion of potato filling inside and fold. Serve hot with sambar and chutney.
