Bisi Bele Bath

Indian Dish

Total Calories: 101 calories
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice
    		•
  • ½ cup toor dal
    		•
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
    		•
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
    		•
  • 2 tbsp bisi bele bath powder
    		•
  • 1 tbsp oil
    		•
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
    		•
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
    		•
  • 2 dried red chilies
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1-
Cook rice and dal together until soft. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, red chilies, and chopped vegetables. Sauté for a few minutes. Add tamarind pulp, bisi bele bath powder, salt, and water. Simmer for 5 minutes. Mix in the cooked rice and dal, stir well, and simmer for another 5 minutes. Serve hot with ghee and fried papad.
