Moongdal Rasam

Lunch

Total Calories: 77 calories
Ingredients:
  • ½ cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
    		•
  • 1 tomato - chopped
    		•
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
    		•
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
    		•
  • 1 green chili - slit
    		•
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
    		•
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
    		•
  • 1 tbsp ghee
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
    		•
Method:
Procedure
Cook moong dal until soft and mash it well. Heat ghee, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, tamarind pulp, and water. Simmer for 5 minutes. Add the mashed dal, salt, and bring to a boil. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with hot rice.
