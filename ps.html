Sambar Rice

Indian Dish

Total Calories: 158 calories
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice
    		•
  • ½ cup toor dal (pigeon pea)
    		•
  • 1 carrot - chopped
    		•
  • 5 beans - chopped
    		•
  • ½ brinjal - chopped
    		•
  • 1 tomato - chopped
    		•
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
    		•
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
    		•
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
    		•
  • 2 tbsp sambar powder
    		•
  • 1 tbsp oil
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
  • green chutney or sandwich chutney as required
    		•
  • butter as required
    		•
  • 12 to 14 bread slices
    		•
Method:
Cook rice and dal together until soft. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chopped vegetables. Sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add tamarind pulp, sambar powder, and salt. Cook until vegetables are soft. Mix in the cooked rice and dal, stir well, and simmer for a few minutes. Serve hot with papad and pickle.
