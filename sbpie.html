Rava kesari

Dessert

Total Calories: 465.3 calories
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
    		•
  • ¾ cup sugar
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • ¼ cup ghee
    		•
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
    		•
  • 10 cashews
    		•
  • 10 raisins
    		•
Method:
Heat ghee, fry cashews and raisins until golden. Remove and set aside. In the same pan, roast semolina until aromatic. Add water, cook on low flame until semolina absorbs it. Mix in sugar, cardamom powder, and remaining ghee. Stir well, add fried nuts, and serve warm
